The Chinese medicine angle

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the Liver is the organ most associated with anger, frustration, resentment, and unfulfilled desires. When these emotions are held over time — particularly when they cannot be expressed freely — Liver Qi stagnates. This stagnation can manifest physically as tension in the chest, ribcage, and shoulders; emotionally as irritability and mood swings; and energetically as a persistent sense of being "stuck."

This class works directly with the Liver meridian using EFT Tapping to move and clear the emotional energy that has accumulated there. The result is often a physical sense of lightness and release — a literal clearing of the weight that has been accumulating in the body.
