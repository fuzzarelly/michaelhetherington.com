The Chinese medicine angle

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the Heart stores the Shen — the mind-spirit — and is responsible for its ability to settle into deep rest at night. When Heart Qi is disturbed (by anxiety, unresolved stress, or excessive stimulation), the Shen cannot "return home" to the Heart at night, resulting in insomnia, restless sleep, and vivid dreams.

The classical window of 1–3am is governed by the Liver. If you consistently wake during this time, it often signals Liver Qi stagnation — unresolved frustration, resentment, or emotional tension that surfaces when the body becomes quiet. The Liver's inability to metabolise these emotions keeps the system in a low-grade state of activation.

This class works with both the Heart and Liver meridians using EFT Tapping — so the approach addresses both the symptom (inability to sleep) and the underlying energetic and emotional patterns driving it.
