The Chinese medicine angle

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the Kidneys govern the body's fundamental life force (Jing) and are associated with fear, deep insecurity, and our relationship with resources and survival. A deep-seated belief that there is not enough — whether of money, love, time, or energy — often reflects a Kidney energy imbalance at the root.

The Kidney meridian also governs the will (Zhi) — our capacity to set intentions and follow through. When Kidney energy is depleted or blocked, it manifests as a lack of drive, difficulty committing to financial goals, or a tendency to self-sabotage at the point of breakthrough.

Working with the Kidney meridian directly through EFT Tapping adds a dimension to money work that purely psychological approaches don't access — releasing the fear at an energetic and somatic level, not just a cognitive one.
