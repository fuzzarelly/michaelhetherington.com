The Chinese medicine angle

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, emotional vulnerability and hyperreactivity are often connected to the Heart and Liver meridians. The Heart governs the Shen — the mind-spirit — and when disturbed, creates restlessness, emotional flooding, and an inability to feel grounded. The Liver is responsible for the smooth flow of Qi and emotions; when it becomes constrained, minor stressors trigger disproportionate reactions.

This class works with both systems — using EFT to clear the stored charge in these meridians, and offering the TCM framework so you understand why you respond the way you do. Understanding the root changes your relationship with reactivity itself.
